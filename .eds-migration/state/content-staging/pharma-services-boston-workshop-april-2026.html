Pediatric Insights: Small Patients, Big Decisions

Developing impactful treatments for younger and older patients is a fine balance between science, safety and ease of administration.

This 'Small Patients, Big Decisions' knowledge sharing session opens up the floor to pharmaceutical experts like you to explore the decisions that shape pediatric programs from early development to commercialization.

Joined by drug development specialist Terry Ernest, expect an evening of genuine connections, real-world solutions and impactful conversations over drinks and appetizers.
