Curtis Campbell

Curtis Campbell shares his experience working at Almac Group and what makes Almac a special place to build a career.

At Almac, we believe in nurturing talent and providing our employees with the tools, training, and opportunities they need to thrive. From day one, our team members are immersed in a culture built on innovation, collaboration, and a genuine commitment to advancing human health.

Almac's Pathways programme connects ambitious individuals with meaningful roles across our global operations — from scientific research and quality assurance to project management and business development.

Whether you're just starting out or are an experienced professional, Almac offers a supportive environment where you can make a real difference. Our people are our greatest asset, and we invest in their development at every stage of their career.
