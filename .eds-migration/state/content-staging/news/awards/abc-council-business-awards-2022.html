ABC Council Business Awards 2022

Almac Group was recognised at the ABC Council Business Awards 2022. The Armagh City, Banbridge and Craigavon (ABC) Council Business Awards celebrate the achievements and successes of local businesses across the council area.

Almac Group is headquartered in Craigavon, Northern Ireland, and is one of the leading employers in the ABC Council area. The company's continued investment in its people, facilities, and innovation has made it a cornerstone of the local economy and a globally recognised pharmaceutical services organisation.

This recognition reflects Almac's commitment to excellence in its operations and its role as a major contributor to the economic development of the region.
